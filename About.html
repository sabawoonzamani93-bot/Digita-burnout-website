Digital Burnout & Screen Fatigue

What is Burnout? Causes & Symptoms Health Effects Prevention Diary

Young adults today spend many hours on phones, laptops, and tablets. While technology helps education and communication, excessive screen use can cause digital burnout and screen fatigue.

Why this matters

  • Reduced concentration
  • Poor sleep quality
  • Mental exhaustion
  • Eye strain

This website helps you understand the causes, effects, and prevention strategies.
