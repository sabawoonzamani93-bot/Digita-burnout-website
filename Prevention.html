Tips to Reduce Digital Burnout

  • Follow the 20-20-20 rule (every 20 min look 20 feet away for 20 sec)
  • Turn off unnecessary notifications
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before sleep
  • Use night mode or blue light filter
  • Take regular outdoor breaks
  • Set daily screen limits

Small changes in habits can greatly improve mental wellbeing.
